INIT

- ModLib.Loader Inits (Preloading process)

  - Entrypoint.Initialize() is called

    - Extension assemblies are located and loaded; Initialize() is called for each one of them.

    - PreInitialize is called

    - CompatibilityManager is initialized; Override files are read and evaluated.
    - Extras is initialized; Static fields are populated for later usage by ModLib and other mods.
    - Core is initialized; Various internal services are initialized and properly configured here.

    - OnInitialize is called

- ModLib is ready for usage by consumers.

---

EXIT

- Entrypoint.Disable() is called

  - PreDisable is called

  - Core is disabled; Services are stopped, and data is saved to disk.
  - Entrypoint class is marked as non-initialized.

  - OnDisable is called

  - Extension assemblies are disabled and unloaded; Disable() is called for each one of them.

- ModLib is disabled, game exits.
